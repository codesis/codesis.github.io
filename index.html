Let's quiz!

Second exam in the course 1dv022

During the course 1dv022 (clientbased webprogramming) we had 3 different examinations. This is the second one. The assignment was to implement a simple quiz. We were given the first question’s url, to which we would send the answer. If the player answered correctly, the response would be the next question’s url. A small and simple RESTful-assignment you might say, as we POST the answer and GET the next url. However the main focus was on the DOM as you may see in the code here.

  • Date: January 2019
  • Course: 1dv022
  • Category: JavaScript, Document Object Model

About me

My journey to this day as a developer

  • 1998-2014

    Childhood

    I can’t be certain on which year I first sat down to play with either my big sister’s gameboy or sneaking to play on my big brother’s nintendo 64, but perhaps I was 4 years old. In either way, I’ve always loved technology and my friends and family has always called me the tech nerd, she who can solve anything! (Well, lets be humble and say: any of their small problems)

  • 2015-2017

    A dream that was not mine

    In January 2015 I started studying to become a nurse. However, as I had my first child Midas in 2017, I realised this was not who I was and this was not my dream. So, after almost 5 (of 6) semesters in nursing school, I quit. And it felt just right.

  • August 2018-June 2020

    Beginning of my journey to become a developer

    Finally! I realised my true dream was in tech. Of course, my friends and family would say. How could I not see this before? But later is better than never! Started studying web developing in August 2018, and took my degree in computer science in June 2020.

  • 2020-

    Today

    I now feel at home but far from complete with the knowledge I have for developing and computer science. I want to learn more and get a better developer than I was yesterday, so that I can create a better product for tomorrow. What may the future hold?
